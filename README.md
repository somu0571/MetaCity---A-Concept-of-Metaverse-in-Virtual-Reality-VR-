# Metacity – Smart Metaverse City

Metacity is an interactive **metaverse-based smart city simulation** built using **Unity, Blender, and C#**. The project creates an immersive 3D virtual environment where users can explore a digitally designed city with realistic infrastructure and dynamic environmental elements.

The objective of Metacity is to demonstrate how **metaverse environments can visualize urban spaces and smart city concepts** through interactive exploration. The city includes animated components and environmental effects that make the virtual world feel more alive and engaging.

## Features

* Interactive **3D virtual city environment**
* **Animated people** moving around the city
* **Moving cars** simulating traffic
* **Flying birds** for environmental realism
* **Dynamic clouds** slowly moving in the sky
* **Ambient sounds** such as dogs barking
* **Background music** to enhance immersion
* **Real-time navigation** allowing users to explore the city freely

## Tech Stack

* **Unity** – Game engine used to build the virtual environment
* **Blender** – 3D modeling and asset creation
* **C#** – Scripting for interactions, animations, and navigation

## Future Scope

* Integration of **AI-driven smart city simulations**
* Addition of **IoT-based virtual systems**
* Enhanced **realistic traffic and pedestrian behavior**
* Expanded **city infrastructure and environment details**
